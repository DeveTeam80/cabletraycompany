Choose Your Cable Tray That Optimizes Growing Conditions in Your Greenhouse - Hutaib Electricals







08-June-2021 || By: Admin




Modern, automated greenhouse equipment can help indoor farmers manage and maintain productive crops. However, implementing the associated power, control and networking infrastructure during a greenhouse can present its own challenges. Whether you’re looking to form systems smarter, automate processes, install advanced lighting, lower energy costs or just boost efficiency, you'll need cable that satisfies the appliance needs and can stand up to greenhouse conditions. When selecting wire and

cable tray

for your indoor growing application, make certain to stay the subsequent factors in mind:


Flexibility Cable trays typically have complex configurations since equipment and cable trays must be positioned to avoid blocking sunlight.



Chemical resistance Whether for plant growth or pest mitigation, greenhouses are teeming with agricultural chemicals and pesticides.



Moisture/humidity/corrosion resistance. Humid conditions in greenhouses can cause condensation forming on equipment or dripping from the ceiling. Puddles also can increase the humidity inside a greenhouse.



UV resistance. Abundant sunlight can cause cable jacketing to degrade.



Temperature Elevated temperatures are common in greenhouses.



Certifications and approvals. Make sure your cable tray satisfies industry and native standards .
