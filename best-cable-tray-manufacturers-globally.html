Complete Range of Cable Tray Solutions


Hutaib Electricals offers a comprehensive portfolio of cable trays and support systems tailored to diverse project requirements:

    Perforated Cable Trays

    Ideal for light- to medium-duty power and data cable support, these trays allow for excellent air ventilation, which prevents overheating and improves cable longevity.

  1. Available in GI, MS, Aluminum, and Stainless Steel
  2. Powder-coated or hot-dip galvanized options
  3. Ideal for commercial, data center, and low-voltage installations

Hutaib Electricals offers heavy-duty and thermally efficient cable tray systems engineered specifically for data centers and IT parks.



    Ladder-Type Cable Trays

    Built for heavy-duty cabling, ladder trays are designed to provide rigid structural support for long cable spans in power-intensive industries.

  1. Strong rungs for maximum load capacity
  2. Available in all standard widths and thicknesses
  3. Customizable bends, risers, and tees



    Raceways & Trunking Systems

    For projects requiring enclosed protection, raceways offer a clean and safe cable pathway while also supporting aesthetic and architectural requirements.



Wire Mesh Cable Trays

Highly flexible and easy to install, wire mesh trays are used in high-tech environments like IT infrastructure and process industries.

  1. Lightweight, corrosion-resistant
  2. Easily cut and configured on-site
  3. Excellent airflow for cable cooling
