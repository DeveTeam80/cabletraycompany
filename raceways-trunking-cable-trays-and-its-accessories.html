Raceways, Trunking Cable Trays And Its Accessories.






2-February-2023 || By: Admin

What Is

Raceway Cable Tray

?:
A raceway is an enclosed conduit that creates a physical channel for electrical wires (also known as a raceway system). Raceways shield wires and cables from physical dangers such as heat, humidity, corrosion, water penetration, and general abrasion.
Raceways, trunking, and accessories play a vital role in managing and protecting electrical wires and cables in commercial, industrial, and residential settings. Hutaib Electricals is a leading manufacturer of high-quality raceways, trunking, and accessories.
In this blog, we will be discussing the various types of raceways, trunking, and their accessories, and the importance of using them.

Types Of Raceways

:
Metal Raceways: Metal raceways are made from materials such as aluminum, steel, or stainless steel and are commonly used in commercial and industrial settings.

Non-Metal Raceways: Non-metal raceways are made from materials such as PVC and are commonly used in residential settings.

Types Of Trunking:
Surface Mounted Trunking: This type of trunking is installed on the surface of a wall or ceiling and is ideal for installations where the cables need to be easily accessible.

Flush Mounted Trunking: This type of trunking is installed within a wall or ceiling and is ideal for installations where the cables need to be hidden.

Accessories For Raceways And Trunking:
Bends: Bends are used to change the direction of the raceway or trunking and make turns.

Tees: Tees are used to split the raceway or trunking into two or more directions. Tees are fittings used in raceway systems to branch off a main raceway and allow for the addition of new circuits. In electrical systems, tees are used to create a T-shaped junction in a raceway, allowing the main raceway to continue in one direction while a branch is taken off in another direction.

Crosses: Crosses are fittings used in raceway systems to connect four raceways at a single point. In electrical systems, crosses are used to create an intersection in the raceway network, allowing multiple raceways to intersect and continue in different directions.

Couplings: Couplings are used to join two sections of the raceway or trunking together.

End Caps: End caps are used to close the end of the raceway or trunking.

The Importance of Using Raceways, Trunking, and Accessories:
Protection of Cables: Raceways and trunking provide protection to the cables from physical damage, dust, and debris. This helps to extend the life of the cables and reduce the need for repairs and replacements.

Ease of Installation: Raceways and trunking are easy to install and can be customized to fit the specific requirements of a project. This makes it easier for electricians to install and maintain the cables.

Improved Cable Management: Raceways and trunking help to organize and manage the cables, making it easier to identify and access them when needed.

Fire Safety: In the event of a fire, metal raceways and trunking help to contain the fire and prevent it from spreading. This helps to minimize the damage and reduce the risk of fire-related incidents.

Aesthetics: Trunking and raceways are available in a range of colors and finishes, making it easy to match them to the d√©cor of a space. This helps to create a clean and tidy appearance, which is important in both residential and commercial settings.

In conclusion, raceways, trunking, and accessories play a crucial role in managing and protecting electrical wires and cables.
