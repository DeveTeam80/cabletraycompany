The Walkable Cable Tray System For Effective Supply Cable Protection







06-June-2022 || By: Admin



The ambient conditions in modern industrial plants pose a significant threat to important power, data, and control cables. A harsh environment is created by soiling, vibrations, and industrial effects. As a result, it's even more critical for machine and plant electrical installations to be able to resist varying loads. Hutaib Electricals

walkable cable tray system

provides a confined compartment for cable routing that protects them from soiling and footfall. The introduction of foreign bodies is prevented by special dust protection features on cover joints.



The cable ducts with coverings are designed to withstand the most extreme loads, which are typically encountered near machines and automated production systems with robotic systems. The cable ducts come in two heights: 100 mm and 110 mm on either side. Six various widths ranging from 100 to 600 mm provide the correct cable duct for any application in machining, mounting, and production. Because of the proportions and materials used in the walkable cable tray system, it complies with the guidelines and criteria of a variety of industries, including numerous automobile manufacturers. Hutaib electricals' walking cable trays are already in use by major players.



Energy, electricity and data are in particularly high demand in automated production lines and system construction applications. To solve these issues, Hutaib Electricals can provide excellent shielding of the vast supply lines. Non-slip cable trays provide the best possible safety and load capacities in the industrial environment.

cable tray system

Trays for cables with proven slide resistance and a displacement area
The coverings' slip and step protection are critical for working safely on walking cable trays. The covers of the Hutaib electrical trays are non-slip and hardwearing thanks to the newly created chequer arrangement, ensuring stability and reducing the danger of accidents in daily usage. According to the current rules, the cable ducts have been tested for R11 slip prevention and have a V10 displacement space.



The slip prevention is evaluated on a ramp with various inclination inclinations and classified into evaluation groups R9 to R13. R10 slip prevention is required in most sectors. The R11 slide prevention on the chequer plate covers for BKRS cable ducts has been tested and is thus acceptable for most industrial settings. If considerable soiling of lubricating substances (e.g. oils, animal fats, etc.) is predicted as a result of the structure's use, the covering surface must have adequate displacement space. This is the only way to ensure safe walking. The Hutaib electrical chequer plate cover with the evaluation group V10 also exceeds the standards of most industries for at least V4 in this area.



Cable ducts provide the best protection for cables.
Heavy loads, stamping feet, dirt, and dust — because of their smart system design, the walking cable trays with covers can handle all of these and more. The cable trays provide great cable protection due to their outstanding mechanical stability. The electromagnetic compatibility of the walking cable ducts is ensured by their adequate shielding. Power, control, and data cables are insulated according to DIN EN 50147 using a separating retainer to ensure electromagnetic compatibility. With a continuous material thickness of 2 mm, the cable tray, cover, and dividing retainers form a highly mechanically stable composite.



The cable tray system is included in the functional equipotential bonding without the use of screws.
Using the KFL clamp spring and AKL connection terminal, the walkable cable tray system can be quickly and without screws integrated into the functional equipotential bonding. According to IEC 61537 - Cable management, this ensures electrical conductivity. The stranded cable is forced into the clamp spring to form the contact, and the clamp spring is simply attached to the cable tray rail without extra drilling. The connecting termination makes it simple to make stranded cable branches and suspensions.



The walking cable tray system is complemented by a variety of accessories. A wide accessory selection complements the anti-slip cable trays for the floor. Whether it's a cover clamp in place of a sash lock, a cover lifter with a clever drilling template, a universal connector with integrated edge protection, or stand-offs for various mounting options, the comprehensive accessories offer a wide range of advantages when mounting the walkable cable trays.



The walking cable tray system offers a wide range of options for each application. This is also true when it comes to mounting.
