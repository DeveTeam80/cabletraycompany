Cable trays & Accessories

offered by Hutaib Electricals

Ladder Cable Trays
Ladder cable trays are made of two parallel side rails connected by rungs to form a ladder-like structure. They are used for heavy-duty applications and can support large cable loads. Hutaib Electricals offers a variety of ladder cable trays with different rung spacing, widths, and heights. They are available in different materials such as stainless steel, galvanized steel, and aluminum, to meet different application requirements.

Perforated Cable Trays


Perforated cable trays are made of metal sheets with holes or slots punched in them. They are used for lighter loads and are commonly used in commercial and industrial buildings. Hutaib Electricals offers a range of perforated cable trays with different hole sizes and shapes, such as round, rectangular, and square. They are available in different materials such as stainless steel, galvanized steel, and aluminum.

Wire Mesh Cable Trays
Wire mesh cable trays are made of welded wire mesh panels and are used for light to medium-duty applications. They are lightweight, easy to install, and provide good ventilation for cables. Hutaib Electricals offers a range of wire mesh cable trays with different mesh sizes and wire diameters. They are available in different materials such as stainless steel, galvanized steel, and aluminum.

Cable Tray Accessories
Hutaib Electricals also offers a range of cable tray accessories, including bends, tees, reducers, couplers, and hangers. These accessories are designed to provide flexibility and convenience when installing cable trays. They are available in different materials such as stainless steel, galvanized steel, and aluminum, and are compatible with Hutaib Electricals cable trays as well as other cable tray brands.
